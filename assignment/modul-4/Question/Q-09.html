Que:-Which property controls the image scroll in the background?

And:-
  • the background-attachment property specifies whether the background image will scroll along with the rest of the elements on the page when the user slides the scroll-bar in the browser or whether the image will stay fixed to the page. the background-attachment property can either be sete to scroll or fixed.
